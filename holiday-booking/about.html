Who We Are

Born From a Passion
for Discovery

Solstice was founded in 2012 by two seasoned travelers who believed that extraordinary trips shouldn't be reserved for the privileged few. They set out to build a travel company that combined the meticulous care of a private concierge with the reach of a global network.

Today, we're a team of 85 passionate travel curators, each with deep expertise in their regions. From the hidden temples of Southeast Asia to the glacier lakes of Patagonia, we know these places because we've lived in them.

Our Journey

Milestones

2012

The Beginning

Solstice was founded in a small London office by Elena and Marcus, with a single mission: make extraordinary travel accessible.

2015

100th Trip

We celebrated our 100th curated journey — a private expedition through the Amazon rainforest for a family of six.

2018

Global Expansion

Offices opened in Dubai, Singapore, and New York, allowing us to serve travelers from every corner of the globe.

2020

Resilience Through Covid

We stood by every single client — processing full refunds and converting bookings, earning our reputation for integrity.

2022

Responsible Travel Pledge

We became a certified B Corp, formalizing our commitment to sustainability, community, and responsible tourism.

2025

20,000 Travelers

A landmark moment — 20,000 happy travelers and counting, each with a story that reminds us why we do what we do.
